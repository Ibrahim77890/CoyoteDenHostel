Services We Provide

Hostel vibes, traditional flair

Feel at home in our rooms decorated with traditional art, textiles, and knicknacks from all over Pakistan. Our Islamabad hostel has both private rooms and dorms at fair prices.

William's Room

Nagarparkar Room

A bright and cheery private room with en-suite bathroom. Has lots of colorful local touches, and access to a small, sunny balcony overlooking a park. Room is equipped with a dual AC-heater.

A spacious dorm room with four beds, each with its own reading light and power outlet. The room has a large window and a view of the Margalla Hills. AC available for an additional fee.
